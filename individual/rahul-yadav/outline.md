# Individual Report Outline: Antigravity

## 1. Problem Statement
*   Current behavioral data moats (Google, Meta) are siloed.
*   Quantum ML for neuroscience is still in lab/pilot phase.
*   No secure, neutral broker exists to bridge population-scale sentiment with neural maps under strict privacy controls (EU AI Act).

## 2. Solution: The Product
*   A PQC-secured neutral data-exchange platform for neural and behavioral signals.
*   The "anti-monopoly" broker that aggregates data for pharma, insurance, or public health models without handing ownership to big tech.
*   Core Value Prop: Trust and compliance (Cybersecurity) enabling predictive cognitive intelligence.

## 3. Technology Stack
*   **Quantum Computing:** Hybrid Quantum-Classical ML for connectomic analysis and pattern recognition.
*   **AI/GenAI:** Trimodal brain encoding (e.g., Meta TRIBE v2).
*   **Cybersecurity:** Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC), Zero-Trust Architecture, differential privacy.

## 4. Business Model & Market (Choose One After Prof Meeting)
*   [ ] Pharma (Licensing cognitive risk models for trial targeting)
*   [ ] Insurance (Cognitive Risk Score APIs)
*   [ ] Public Health (Government compliance dashboards)

## 5. Strategic Advantage
*   Leverages existing behavioral data streams (Google Trends) combined with quantum ML to bypass the need for physical brain scans on every individual.
*   Cyber-by-design ensures regulatory resilience as laws like the US MIND Act and EU AI Act become stringent.
