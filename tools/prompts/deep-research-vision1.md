# Perplexity Deep Research Prompt - Vision 1

## Prompt

```
I am an IMBA student at IE Business School writing an individual report proposing a new product or service that creates a strategic competitive advantage for a company or industry. The product must be enabled by one or more of these technologies: AI, Generative AI, Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, Blockchain/DLT, or Quantum Computing, with Cybersecurity as a mandatory enabling capability.

I need you to conduct deep research across the following dimensions:

1. PRODUCT OPPORTUNITY LANDSCAPE
For each of the 6 technologies (AI, Gen AI, AR, VR, Blockchain, Quantum Computing), identify:
- The most promising new product/service opportunities being actively researched or piloted RIGHT NOW (2024-2026)
- Industries or companies where these technologies are creating or about to create a genuine strategic inflection point (not just incremental improvement)
- Specific gaps in the market where no dominant solution exists yet

2. WHERE TO FIND FRONTIER RESEARCH
List the best sources to monitor for each technology:
- Academic/research institutions publishing applied work (MIT, Stanford, etc.)
- Industry reports (Gartner, McKinsey, WEF, etc.)
- Startup ecosystems (Y Combinator, a16z, etc.)
- Government/regulatory signals (EU AI Act, NIST, etc.)
- Journals and preprint servers (arXiv, Nature, etc.)

3. UNDEREXPLORED INTERSECTIONS
Identify product opportunities that emerge specifically at the intersection of two or more of these technologies (combinations that are technically credible today but haven't been built into a dominant product yet).

4. CYBERSECURITY ANGLE
For each technology, what is the most strategically important cybersecurity product opportunity (not just a bolt-on, but cybersecurity as the core value proposition)?

5. REAL COMPANY PAIN POINTS
Which industries and specific large companies have publicly stated strategic problems (in earnings calls, annual reports, or press) that one of these technologies could solve as a new product, with evidence?

For each opportunity identified, structure your output as:
- Technology used
- Industry/company
- The unsolved problem
- What the product would do
- Why now (what makes 2025-2026 the right moment)
- Key source/evidence

Be specific. Avoid generic "AI will transform X" statements. I need concrete, defensible product ideas grounded in current research and real company pain points.
```

## How to Run
- Use Perplexity with Focus: Academic mode and Deep Research enabled
- Expected output: comprehensive frontier product opportunities report
- File the output in: `research/vision1/sources.md` and topic-specific notes
