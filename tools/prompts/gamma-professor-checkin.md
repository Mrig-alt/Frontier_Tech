# Gamma Prompt — Professor Check-In Presentation

Paste the following into Gamma.app → "Generate" → "Presentation" → Paste as prompt:

---

Create a sleek, dark-mode, 5-slide presentation for a professor check-in meeting. Use a futuristic tech aesthetic with deep navy/black backgrounds, electric blue and purple accent gradients, and clean sans-serif typography. The tone is confident but consultative — we are presenting progress and asking for guidance, not pitching a final product.

**SLIDE 1 — TITLE**
Title: "Antigravity"
Subtitle: "A Quantum-Ready Behavioral Neuroscience Platform"
Small tagline: "Transforming cognitive intelligence with frontier technologies"
Bottom details:
- Course: IMBA 25S Frontier Technologies
- Professor: Casimiro Juanes
- Status: Research phase — seeking guidance on industry direction

**SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM**
Title: "The Strategic Opportunity"
Present these 4 bullet points on the left side:
1. 95% of enterprise GenAI pilots fail to deliver measurable ROI (McKinsey / Menlo Ventures)
2. AI pilots are fragmented across business units with no unified governance
3. EU AI Act (2026-2027) classifies neural data products as high-risk — requiring robust cybersecurity, risk management, and audit trails
4. No dominant product connects behavioral data + quantum ML + neuroscience

On the right side, display this quote prominently in italic with a highlight or callout box:
"Google is already sitting on the most detailed map of human anxiety ever assembled. The question is who builds the bridge to the clinic before they do."

**SLIDE 3 — THE PLATFORM**
Title: "Antigravity Platform"
One-liner: "A cybersecurity-first neutral broker that aggregates consented behavioral and neural signals, feeds hybrid quantum-ML models, and sells causal cognitive intelligence."

Show a 4-layer vertical stack diagram or 4 cards:
- Layer 1 — Behavioral Data Ingestion: Google Trends + social media emotional signals. Processes 80% of global search volume (136B monthly visits). Outputs structured behavioral vectors capturing intent, emotion, and decision-making across all languages.
- Layer 2 — Neural Reference & Mapping: Meta TRIBE v2 trimodal brain encoding model. Trained on 500+ hours of fMRI data from 700+ individuals. Zero-shot generalization to new subjects and unseen languages.
- Layer 3 — Quantum ML Engine: IBM Quantum (Willow chip, 1000+ qubits). Hybrid quantum-classical sentiment analysis. Quantum Fourier Transform processes teravoxel connectome datasets with ~30% signal-to-noise improvement. Inspired by IBM + Inclusive Brains BMI study (June 2025).
- Layer 4 — Security & Governance: Post-Quantum Cryptography (NIST roadmap). Zero-Trust Architecture + Differential Privacy. EU AI Act (2026-2027) and US MIND Act (2025) compliant. No data moves to production without passing this layer.

**SLIDE 4 — ROADMAP & EVIDENCE**
Title: "Three Horizons"
Show a horizontal timeline with 3 phases:
- H1 (Now): Government entry via DSA / EU AI Act compliance tool
- H2 (12-24 months): Quantum ML biomarker scoring — pharma and insurer contracts
- H3 (24+ months): New standard of care — pharma trials, cognitive risk pricing, population health

Below the timeline, show a small "Key Evidence" section:
- IBM + Inclusive Brains joint study on quantum BMI classification (June 2025)
- Meta TRIBE v2: 2-3× improvement over previous neural encoding methods
- Google controls 80% of global search — the largest behavioral signal corpus ever assembled
- BCI market: $2.94B in 2025 → projected $12.40B by 2034

**SLIDE 5 — GUIDANCE NEEDED**
Title: "Open Decisions"
Show 3 option cards side by side, each with an icon:
- 🔴 Pharma (Roche, J&J) — trial cohort targeting, biomarker discovery
- 🟡 Insurance (AXA) — cognitive risk scoring, population health pricing
- 🔵 Public Health (NHS, EU Commission) — causal harm dashboard

Below the cards, list 2 additional questions:
- Does the chosen industry count as strategic transformation or disruption per the group brief?
- How much differentiation is needed between our group presentation and individual reports?

Footer: "Next steps: Lock industry direction → Finalize deck structure → Assign presenters → Write speaking script"

Design preferences: Dark mode, futuristic, gradients (navy to purple), minimal text per slide, use icons and diagrams over bullet walls. Professional but bold.
