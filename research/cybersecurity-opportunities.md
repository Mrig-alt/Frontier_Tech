# Cybersecurity as Core Product Opportunity

## 1. AI / GenAI: AI Supply‑Chain Security Platform
*   **The unsolved problem:** Research demonstrates that a small number of poisoned documents can significantly compromise LLM behavior; attackers target AI training pipelines and model registries.
*   **Product concept:** A "SaaS for AI supply-chain security": monitors training data sources, performs data hygiene/provenance checks, scans model registries for anomalies, and integrates with MLOps platforms. Includes incident-response playbooks and regulatory-ready reporting.

## 2. AR / VR: Secure XR Device and Session Management for Enterprises
*   **The unsolved problem:** XR devices introduce new endpoints, sensors, and data types that can be abused to exfiltrate data or physically manipulate users; current EDR/MDM tools are not tailored to XR.
*   **Product concept:** An XR security platform that manages headset identities, enforces secure configurations, monitors sessions for abnormal behavior, and integrates with SIEM/SOAR.

## 3. Blockchain: Smart‑Contract and Web3 Threat‑Hunting Platform
*   **The unsolved problem:** Smart-contract vulnerabilities and address-poisoning attacks drive major losses, and AI agents can now autonomously exploit them.
*   **Product concept:** A security platform that continuously scans on-chain contracts for known patterns and AI-discovered zero-days, simulates attacks, and recommends mitigations.

## 4. Quantum: PQC Migration and Monitoring Suite
*   **The unsolved problem:** Organizations must replace vulnerable cryptography ahead of quantum breakthroughs; migration is complex and lengthy.
*   **Product concept:** A suite that inventories cryptographic assets, prioritizes PQC migration, tests new algorithms, and monitors progress against regulatory/industry timelines.
