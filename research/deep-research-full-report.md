## Executive Summary
From 2024–2026, AI, generative AI, AR/VR, blockchain/DLT, and quantum computing are moving from experimentation to deployment in specific, high‑value workflows, while cybersecurity risk becomes a board‑level constraint on adoption.

*   Enterprise gen‑AI spending is exploding yet over 90% of pilots still fail to move the P&L.
*   AR/VR are proving ROI in industrial maintenance and training.
*   Real‑world asset (RWA) tokenization is inflecting from pilots to institutional deployment.
*   Quantum computing is still early, but hybrid quantum‑classical optimization is already being piloted in logistics and finance.
*   Across all six technologies, AI‑augmented cyber threats, smart‑contract exploits, metaverse/XR vulnerabilities, and post‑quantum cryptography migration create space for cybersecurity‑centric products.

## 1. Product Opportunity Landscape by Technology

### 1.1 Applied AI (beyond GenAI)
**Opportunity A1: AI Platformization for Fragmented Enterprise Pilots**
*   **Technology used:** Applied AI, MLOps, policy orchestration; security as core design.
*   **Industry:** Large enterprises (global banks, telcos).
*   **The unsolved problem:** Pilots are fragmented across business units, run on separate RAG stacks, and lack unified governance.
*   **The product:** A secure AI control plane that standardizes how models are exposed, enforcing PII redaction, data residency, and workload observability.

**Opportunity A2: AI‑Driven Payment and Treasury Operations Optimization**
*   **Technology used:** Predictive AI for cash‑flow and routing.
*   **Industry:** Large multinationals, global treasuries.
*   **The product:** Aggregates multi-bank data via APIs, uses AI for routing, anomaly detection, and intraday liquidity optimization.

### 1.2 Generative AI
**Opportunity G1: GenAI for Drug Discovery & Trial Design with Built‑In Compliance**
*   **Technology used:** Generative models for molecule design, predictive AI for ADMET.
*   **Industry:** Pharma and biotech.
*   **The product:** End-to-end GxP-ready GenAI platform for drug discovery with built-in differential privacy and access controls.

**Opportunity G2: Secure GenAI Copilots for Regulated Functions**
*   **Technology used:** GenAI with RAG, policy engines.
*   **Industry:** Banks, insurers, critical infrastructure.
*   **The product:** "Compliance-first" copilots trained on internal policies with integrated data-loss prevention (DLP).

### 1.3 Augmented Reality (AR)
