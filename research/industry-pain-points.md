# Real Company and Industry Pain Points

## 1. Banking and Financial Services
*   **Technology used:** AI/GenAI, Blockchain, Cybersecurity.
*   **Industry/company:** European and global banks.
*   **The unsolved problem:** Legacy systems, siloed data, and payment fragmentation lead to high operational costs, slow product launches, and regulatory risk. Core platforms often exceed 25–30 years in age.
*   **What the product would do:** A combined AI + DLT treasury and data platform consolidating fragmented data, using tokenized assets and real-time payments to reduce settlement friction with embedded cyber controls.
*   **Why now:** Regulatory pressure on resilience, plus the rise of RWA tokenization and open banking.

## 2. Pharma and Biotech
*   **Technology used:** GenAI, AI, AR/VR, Cybersecurity.
*   **Industry/company:** Large pharma and AI-native biotechs (e.g., Insilico Medicine).
*   **The unsolved problem:** Drug discovery and clinical development are costly and slow. Companies invest heavily in AI but operate under strict regulatory and data-protection constraints.
*   **What the product would do:** Secure GenAI discovery platforms and AR/VR training for lab procedures, maintaining compliance and data security.
*   **Why now:** Rapid growth in GenAI-based discovery; early AI-enabled firms are scaling pipelines.

## 3. Logistics and Transportation
*   **Technology used:** AI, Quantum, AR/VR, Cybersecurity.
*   **Industry/company:** Logistics firms, postal services, rail operators.
*   **The unsolved problem:** Complex optimization problems and safety requirements; pressure to cut emissions while managing workforce safety.
*   **What the product would do:** Quantum-enhanced logistics optimization, VR safety training, and AR maintenance secured with PQC-ready encryption.
*   **Why now:** Early quantum pilots show promise; VR/AR training is delivering measurable outcomes.

## 4. Industrial and Energy Sectors
*   **Technology used:** AR, VR, GenAI, AI, Cybersecurity.
*   **Industry/company:** Utilities, energy companies, renewables (e.g., Avangrid using VR).
*   **The unsolved problem:** Aging workforce, complex maintenance, and safety risks; lack of integrated, secure AR/VR platforms that scale.
*   **What the product would do:** Industrial AR Work Assist and VR training platforms rooted in zero-trust principles for OT, with GenAI for procedure generation.
*   **Why now:** Industrial AR/VR adoption is inflecting; cyber-physical security guidance stresses the need to secure new XR devices.
