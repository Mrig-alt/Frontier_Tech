# Antigravity Platform: Technical Architecture & Tech Stack

To build the Predictive Cognitive Intelligence Platform (Vision 1), the architecture requires integrating population-scale behavioral data, advanced neural encoding, quantum machine learning, and strict cybersecurity controls.

## 1. Behavioral Data Ingestion Layer
*   **Core Data Moat:** Google Search Corpus / Google Trends.
*   **Scale:** Handling roughly 80% of global search (approx. 136 billion monthly visits).
*   **Function:** Ingests high-dimensional, cross-language, global-scale datasets to capture intent, emotion, and decision-making at a population level.

## 2. Neural Reference & Mapping Layer
*   **Core Model:** Meta TRIBE v2 (Trimodal Brain Encoding Model).
*   **Training Baseline:** Trained on 500+ hours of fMRI data from 700+ individuals.
*   **Function:** Maps behavioral inputs (video, audio, text) to expected neural responses. Provides zero-shot generalization to new people and unseen languages.

## 3. Quantum ML Processing Engine
*   **Hardware:** IBM Quantum Infrastructure (1000+ qubit systems, e.g., the Willow chip).
*   **Algorithms:** 
    *   **Hybrid Quantum-Classical Solvers:** Used for complex sentiment analysis on large-scale datasets, outperforming classical ML in detecting subtle cross-linguistic emotional signals.
    *   **Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT):** Deployed to process teravoxel connectome datasets and reduce signal-to-noise ratios by ~30% (similar to applications in rapid MRI reconstruction).
*   **Function:** Acts as the core classification engine, bridging the behavioral data and the neural maps to predict cognitive states. Leverages algorithms similar to the June 2025 IBM + Inclusive Brains joint study for BMI classification.

## 4. Security, Compliance & Governance Layer
*   **Cryptographic Standard:** Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) aligned with NIST migration roadmaps.
*   **Architecture:** Zero-Trust Architecture and Differential Privacy.
*   **Regulatory Compliance:**
    *   **EU AI Act (2026-2027):** Built-in risk management, logging, and audit trails to comply with "high-risk" classifications for neural/biometric systems.
    *   **US MIND Act (2025):** Protects mental privacy and prevents unauthorized corporate commodification of neural signals.
*   **Function:** Ensures the platform acts as a secure, neutral data-exchange broker that can be trusted by governments, pharma, and insurers.
