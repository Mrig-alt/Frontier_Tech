## Business Model Concepts

### Option A: Precision Psychiatry Intelligence Platform
- Sell AI-inferred population-level mental health signals to pharma R&D and clinical trial recruiters
- Value prop: replace expensive, slow clinical screening with real-time signal detection
- Revenue: SaaS per-seat licensing + data API access fees
- Key risk: regulatory exposure under MIND Act / GDPR Article 9 (special category data)

### Option B: Cognitive Risk Insurance Scoring
- License causal cognitive signal models to insurers (AXA, Swiss Re) for underwriting
- Replace self-reported mental health history with objective behavioral signals
- Revenue: per-query API pricing, white-label risk engine
- Key risk: actuarial fairness challenges, potential discrimination liability

### Option C: Neutral Broker / Cybersecurity-First Data Exchange
- Act as neutral intermediary: aggregate behavioral + neural signals, anonymize, certify
- Sell access to pharma, government, and academic institutions under strict data governance
- Differentiator: zero raw data retention, only causal model outputs leave the platform
- Revenue: data access subscriptions + certification fees
- Key risk: trust model depends entirely on security credibility — one breach = game over

## Core Mechanism: Why Quantum Unlocks This

### The Brain Analogy
- Classical ML = linear trial-and-error: try one path, evaluate, adjust, repeat
- Human brain = simultaneous parallel exploration of many hypotheses at once
- Quantum ML = qubits in superposition explore many states simultaneously — far closer to how the brain actually processes information
- This is not a metaphor; it is the specific computational property that makes population-scale behavioral modeling feasible

### Why 10–20 Years of Google Trends Data
- Behavioral signals must be longitudinal to capture how human thought patterns evolve, not just snapshot correlations
- 20 years of search trends encode how entire populations have responded to events, crises, economic cycles, and cultural shifts
- Short-term data catches noise; long-term data reveals causal structure in human cognition
- Google Trends is a proxy for collective human attention — the closest thing to a population-level thought record that exists

### The Training Pipeline (Simplified)
1. Input: 20 years of Google Trends + social media sentiment signals, cross-linguistic
2. Quantum ML layer: hybrid quantum-classical model identifies high-dimensional pattern clusters
3. Cross-reference: overlay with neural reference maps (Meta TRIBE v2 fMRI, Harvard brain atlas)
4. Output: predictive model — given current behavioral signals, what neural/cognitive state is the population trending toward?

## Key Definitions for Presentation

**Causal Cognitive Intelligence**: AI outputs that identify *why* a population sub-segment shows a neural/behavioral pattern, not just *that* a correlation exists. Enables actionable clinical or policy intervention.

**Population-Level Connectomics**: Deriving statistical brain connectivity maps from aggregate behavioral data rather than individual fMRI scans. Lower cost, higher scale, lower individual privacy risk.

**Quantum Advantage in Neuroscience**: Quantum ML outperforming classical ML specifically on high-dimensional, entangled datasets — exactly the structure of multimodal brain + behavioral signal fusion.

**Neutral Broker Model**: Platform that processes sensitive data without retaining raw inputs, certified by independent auditors, with cryptographic proofs of compliance. Analogous to SWIFT in financial messaging.
