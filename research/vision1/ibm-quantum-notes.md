# IBM Quantum Computing & Neuroscience

## Core Initiative
*   **IBM + Inclusive Brains Joint Study (June 2025):** Applying quantum machine learning (QML) specifically to brain-machine interface (BMI) classification.
*   **Objective:** Testing hundreds of thousands of ML algorithm combinations to identify the most efficient approaches for interpreting complex brain activity.
*   **Status:** This is active, commercially motivated research from two companies with deployed quantum hardware and clinical neurotech applications.

## Technical Capabilities
*   **Hardware:** IBM possesses the most mature quantum hardware, utilizing systems with 1000+ qubits (e.g., the Willow chip).
*   **Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT):** QFT has already been shown to reduce MRI reconstruction times from hours to minutes while improving signal-to-noise ratios by ~30%.
*   **Hybrid Solvers:** Hybrid quantum-classical sentiment analysis outperforms classical ML on large-scale datasets, particularly in accuracy and the ability to detect subtle cross-linguistic emotional signals.
*   **Data Processing:** Quantum-enhanced AI processes teravoxel electron microscopy connectome datasets with 99.7% precision using quantum-optimized feature matching (Argonne National Laboratory).
