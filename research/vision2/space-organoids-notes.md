# Space Organoids Notes (Vision 2)

## Context
- Space organoids = miniaturized, 3D cell cultures grown in microgravity
- Used to study how human biology behaves in space conditions
- Critical for understanding long-duration space mission health risks
- Agentic AI + organoids = autonomous experimentation platform

## Connection to Vision 2 Thesis
- Agentic AI needs a testbed for biological experimentation
- Space organoids provide a controlled environment to test AI-driven biological interventions
- Brain mapping provides the "expected outcome" reference
- Cybersecurity-first because organoid data = human biological data

## Research Areas
- Organoid growth in microgravity (ISS, planned space stations)
- How cells differentiate differently in space vs Earth
- Implications for astronaut health on Mars missions
- Potential for drug testing in simulated space conditions

## Industry Direction: Space Medicine
- NASA/ESA are the primary customers
- Autonomous body support for long-duration missions
- Mars mission communication delay (20 min) = human-in-the-loop is impossible
- Agentic AI must manage physiological systems autonomously
- Organoids are the test platform; brain maps are the control reference

- Is space medicine a more defensible niche than terrestrial applications?
