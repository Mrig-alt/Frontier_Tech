# Vision 2: Agentic Embodiment Platform

## The Thesis
Cursor proved agentic AI can solve tasks autonomously in software. The next domain is biology. Agentic AI learns to instruct cells and organoid systems → brain mapping gives us human control logic → cybersecurity-first because working with human body data requires trust from day one → endgame is autonomous body support for long-duration space missions.

## The Predator Line
"Cursor proved AI can figure out software autonomously. Vision 2 asks whether the same loop can figure out biology — and if it can, whoever builds that platform controls the infrastructure for human survival in space."

## Three Industry Directions
🔴 **Space medicine:** (NASA/ESA — autonomous body support)
🟡 **Biotech/synthetic biology:** (agentic drug trial platform)
🔵 **Embodied robotics:** (Boston Dynamics / Hyundai — hybrid body platform)
