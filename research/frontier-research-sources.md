# Where to Find Frontier Research

## AI and Generative AI
*   **Academic:** MIT CSAIL, MIT Schwarzman College of Computing (e.g., *The GenAI Divide* report), Stanford HAI, Berkeley BAIR.
*   **Industry:** McKinsey's State of AI, Gartner and IDC enterprise spending trends.
*   **Startup ecosystems:** AI 100 lists, Y Combinator's hard tech and AI portfolios.
*   **Regulation:** EU AI Act, NIST AI Risk Management Frameworks.
*   **Journals:** arXiv (cs.LG, cs.AI).

## AR / VR (XR)
*   **Academic:** University labs on human-computer interaction (HCI).
*   **Industry:** Consulting and vendor whitepapers on AR/VR industrial efficiency.
*   **Startup ecosystems:** YC AR/VR portfolio.
*   **Regulation:** Emerging XR safety and cybersecurity guidelines.
*   **Journals:** Medical and occupational health journals.

## Blockchain / DLT
*   **Academic:** University blockchain centers collaborating with financial institutions.
*   **Industry:** RWA tokenization reports, Web3 security advisories (OWASP).
*   **Startup ecosystems:** RWA and institutional DeFi startups.
*   **Regulation:** MAS Project Guardian, EU MiCA, US congressional hearings.
*   **Journals:** Cryptology ePrint.

## Quantum Computing
*   **Academic:** University research groups and national labs working on quantum optimization.
*   **Industry:** QED-C sector reports.
*   **Startup ecosystems:** Deep-tech analyses (e.g., SEEQC, Multiverse Computing).
*   **Regulation:** NIST and national cybersecurity bodies on PQC migration roadmaps.
*   **Journals:** arXiv (quant-ph).

## Cybersecurity (cross‑cutting)
*   **Academic:** Security labs publishing on AI model poisoning and XR/IoT threats.
*   **Industry:** Annual cybersecurity trend reports.
*   **Regulation:** National strategies on AI and cybersecurity.
*   **Journals:** Security conferences and preprints.
