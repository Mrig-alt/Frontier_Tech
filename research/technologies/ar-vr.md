# Augmented Reality (AR) & Virtual Reality (VR) Opportunities

## Opportunity AR1: Cyber‑Safe AR Work Assistant for Industrial Maintenance
*   **Technology used:** AR (headsets/tablets), IoT data integration, AI/GenAI for instructions, security-hardened edge/cloud stack.
*   **Industry/company:** Energy, manufacturing, utilities, process industries.
*   **The unsolved problem:** AR pilots show 40% faster repair times, yet deployments remain siloed and lack standardized security models. AR platforms connecting to SCADA/OT data expand the attack surface.
*   **What the product would do:** Deliver a hardened AR Work Assistant platform overlaying live sensor data and step-by-step guidance. Secure identity, local processing for OT data, and zero-trust network segmentation. GenAI transforms manuals into context-aware procedures with compliance logging.
*   **Why now:** AR solutions are moving to broader industrial use; 5G and edge compute enable low-latency. Cybersecurity research shows enterprises urgently need XR-native security controls.

## Opportunity AR2: Regulated‑Environment AR for Compliance‑Critical Procedures
*   **Technology used:** AR with workflow engines, computer vision, and compliance-grade logging.
*   **Industry/company:** Pharma manufacturing, aviation MRO, nuclear infrastructure.
*   **The unsolved problem:** Complex procedures are governed by strict SOPs; paper-based checklists are error-prone. No dominant AR platform exists for regulated operations.
*   **What the product would do:** AR system that enforces step sequencing via computer-vision confirmation and collects time-stamped evidence. Embeds cyber-resilience: firmware-signed headsets, strong identity, and tamper-evident logs (potentially blockchain).
*   **Why now:** AR maturity is improving. XR security guidance warns unprotected AR headsets are a major threat vector.

## Opportunity VR1: Enterprise VR Training Platform for High‑Risk Work with Safety Analytics
*   **Technology used:** VR simulations, AI analytics, optional GenAI scenario generation, security-hardened content delivery.
*   **Industry/company:** Healthcare, energy, aviation, emergency response.
*   **The unsolved problem:** VR training pilots improve outcomes but struggle to scale, integrate with LMS, and manage device/biometric data privacy.
*   **What the product would do:** Verticalized VR training platform with validated scenarios and performance analytics. Strong cybersecurity: secure headset enrollment, encrypted content distribution, strict control of biometric data, and incident-response playbooks.
*   **Why now:** VR hardware is maturing. Enterprises demand ROI at scale and XR security standardization.

## Opportunity VR2: VR‑Based Soft‑Skill and Risk‑Scenario Training
*   **Technology used:** VR, behavioral analytics, GenAI-driven NPCs, secure data handling.
*   **Industry/company:** Retail banks, insurers, customer-service intensive businesses.
*   **The unsolved problem:** Training for high-stress situations is inconsistent. Soft-skill performance is hard to measure objectively.
*   **What the product would do:** VR scenarios with GenAI dynamic dialogue to simulate fraudsters and crisis situations. Captures behavioral signals to assess risk awareness while ensuring privacy compliance.
*   **Why now:** AI-driven phishing and deepfake social engineering require sophisticated, experiential frontline training.
