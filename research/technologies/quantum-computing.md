# Quantum Computing Opportunities

## Opportunity Q1: Quantum‑Enhanced Logistics Optimization as a Service
*   **Technology used:** Hybrid quantum-classical solvers for routing, scheduling, inventory; delivered via cloud APIs.
*   **Industry/company:** Transportation and logistics firms, postal operators, large manufacturers.
*   **The unsolved problem:** Logistics optimization is computationally challenging. Quantum hardware is early, but hybrid algorithms and "quantum-inspired" solvers are showing promise in pilots (e.g., train scheduling).
*   **What the product would do:** Offer a quantum-enhanced optimization service via API. Runs hybrid solvers, returns optimized plans, and provides interpretable outputs (cost/time/emissions trade-offs). Embeds data confidentiality and PQC-ready encryption.
*   **Why now:** QED-C identifies logistics as a near-term domain for quantum impact. Climate targets and fuel costs create strong incentives for efficiency.
*   **Key sources/evidence:** QED-C report on quantum for transportation; arXiv overviews.

## Opportunity Q2: Post‑Quantum Cryptography (PQC) Discovery, Inventory and Migration Platform
*   **Technology used:** Cryptographic inventory tools, risk analytics, PQC libraries.
*   **Industry/company:** Any organization with long-lived sensitive data (banks, governments, healthcare, cloud providers).
*   **The unsolved problem:** Migration to PQC will take years. "Harvest now, decrypt later" threats make long-term data vulnerable. Organizations lack visibility into where cryptography is used across their systems.
*   **What the product would do:** A PQC readiness and migration platform that discovers cryptographic assets, maps them to business processes, and scores quantum risk. Offers automated recommendations and playbooks aligned with NIST.
*   **Why now:** NIST and national bodies emphasize PQC migration must begin immediately. Early movers are deploying PQC in production TLS flows.
*   **Key sources/evidence:** NIST NCCoE guidance, PQC Migration Roadmaps, Cloudflare's state of the post-quantum Internet.
